Remove Crosshair

toggle the visibility of your crosshair. This can be useful if you're ever in need of taking in-game screenshots.

Mute Team

This bind will mute your teammates voice communition.

Clear Decals

Decals are any effects added to the walls and ground of map as a result of gameplay: blood, graffitis, etc. This bind will instantly clear all decals.

Toggle Hands

Will switch your gun position between your left and right hand. Typically used to help prevent your gun model reducing visability.

Jump Throw

Must be placed in your autoexec Pull pin and throw perfect jump throws every time

Net_graph

The net_graph is a useful tool, showing for statistics at the bottom of your screen. however, in turn adds unnecessary clutter to your game and can be distracting. This bind will make it so that when you click a key, the net_graph menu will show, and when you let go it will hide.

Zoom Radar

Zoom into your radar! Pressing it 4 times will loop the size back round to where it was originally.
